Legal Guide

Greek recreational fishing regulations

Mandatory Reporting

Since 2026, recreational sea fishers in Greece must register and report certain catches via the official national digital system.

Why report?
Helps scientists manage fish stocks sustainably
Protects endangered and overfished species
Avoids potential fines and legal issues
How to report
