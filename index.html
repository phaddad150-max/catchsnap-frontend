Stay Legal in Greece
Mandatory since 2026
Important: Recreational sea fishers in Greece must register and report certain catches via the official national digital system.
Why report?
  • • Helps scientists manage fish stocks
  • • Protects species like the one you just caught
  • • Avoids potential fines
How to report (official steps):
  1. Go to the official national recreational fishing portal or app.
  2. Complete digital registration (free, annual).
  3. Report your catch electronically — same day for many species.

This app guides you — it does not replace the official system.
