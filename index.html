Legal Guide
Greek recreational fishing regulations
Important: Recreational sea fishers in Greece must register and report certain catches via the official national digital system.
Why report?
  • Helps scientists manage fish stocks sustainably
  • Protects endangered and overfished species
  • Avoids potential fines and legal issues
How to report
  1. Go to the official national recreational fishing portal
  2. Complete digital registration (free, annual)
  3. Log catches electronically — same day for many species
